Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Project Name
NEAT Engagements Website

Version

1.0

Overview

NEAT Engagements requires a modern, professional, and responsive corporate website that serves as both an informational platform and a lead-generation tool. The website will showcase the company's identity, history, and services while providing potential clients and applicants with a streamlined method of contacting the company and submitting inquiries.

The website should emphasize trust, professionalism, accessibility, and ease of communication.

Project Goals

Business Goals

* Establish a professional online presence.
* Showcase company services and history.
* Generate qualified leads through inquiry forms.
* Allow applicants and potential clients to contact the company directly.
* Automate inquiry notifications via email.

User Goals

* Learn about NEAT Engagements.
* Explore available services.
* Contact the company easily.
* Submit service inquiries.
* Apply or express interest in working with the company.
* Receive confirmation after form submission.

Core Functional Requirements

Inquiry Submission System

Client Inquiry Form

When a visitor submits an inquiry:

Store:

* Full Name
* Company Name
* Email
* Phone Number
* Subject
* Message
* Timestamp

Email Notification

Upon submission:

Send email to:

* company email address

Include:

* Full Name
* Company Name
* Email Address
* Phone Number
* Subject
* Inquiry Message
* Submission Date

Example Subject:

"New Inquiry Received - NEAT Engagements Website"


Automatic Confirmation Email

After successful inquiry submission:

Send automated confirmation email to the user.

Example Subject:

"Thank You for Contacting NEAT Engagements"

Email Body:

Thank you for contacting NEAT Engagements.

We have received your inquiry and our team will review it shortly.

A representative will contact you as soon as possible.

Regards,
NEAT Engagements Team

Applicant Submission System

When an applicant submits a form:

Send email notification to company email containing:

* Applicant Name
* Email
* Phone Number
* Desired Position
* Resume Attachment
* Message

Send confirmation email to applicant.
